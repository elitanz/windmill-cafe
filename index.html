A no-frills griddle that locals have loved for decades.

The Windmill is the kind of place where the booths are red, the coffee cup never goes empty, and the cook waves from the pass-through. It’s been part of Savage forever — a low cinderblock spot on Highway 13 where troopers, truckers, and families all squeeze in for the same thing: a big, honest, homestyle breakfast.

No trends, no fuss. Just hash browns done right, bacon how you asked for it, and a griddle that’s been seasoned by the years. Open early, every single day.
